# Pet-Shelter (C++ Project)

Helping animals find a loving home, one record at a time. PetShelter is a simple system to collect, organize, and manage information about animals in shelters. Keep track of pets, their needs, and their adoption status efficiently.

Features
Add, update, and remove animal records
Track species, age, breed, health, and adoption status
Search & filter animals for easy management
Lightweight, easy to extend, and beginner-friendly
Usage
Launch PetShelter
Add animals with their relevant details
Update or remove records as needed
Search or filter animals to find adoption-ready pets
